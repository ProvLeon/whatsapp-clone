# Notification Sound

To add a notification sound to your WhatsApp clone:

1. Download or create a short notification sound file (MP3 format recommended)
2. Name it `notification.mp3`
3. Place it in this `public` folder

The app will automatically play this sound when new messages arrive (if sound notifications are enabled).

## Recommended sound characteristics:
- Duration: 0.5-1 second
- Format: MP3
- File size: Under 50KB
- Style: Short, pleasant chime or pop sound

## Free notification sounds:
- https://notificationsounds.com/
- https://freesound.org/
